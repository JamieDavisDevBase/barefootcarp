Bare Foot Carpentry

Purpose

The purpose of this project will be to have something to showcase in my professional portfolio.

Audience

This site is designed for anyone digging into my portfolio.

Goal

The primary goal is to give my portfolio more depth upon DDC graduation. A secondary goal of creating this website is for my side hustle, should I ever decide to step up production.

Persona

Name

Sally Sassafrass

Age

62

Profession

Retired flight attendant

Technology

Uses Windows 10 on her Dell OptiPlex 3080 and Android on her Samsung Galaxy S10e.

Attitudes and Behavior

Plenty of time and in no hurry, she wants to find a gift she can give and also buy for herself that includes a picture of her grandchildren (who helped her set up her phone and computer).

Frustrations and Needs

Though she may have time on her hands, she has no interest in complicated check-out processes or hefty internet searches. She wants the right gift, but also desires a streamlined website from which to order it from.

Goals

Sally needs a gift, but will not spend more than 10 minutes on a website, and definitely no more than 5 minutes in the purchase process.

User Story

"As a less than casual user, I need to be able to easily navigate a website without any unpleasant surprises."
